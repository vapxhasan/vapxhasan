FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

Can you combine development, design, and AI work in one project? ▾
Yes — that's actually most of what I do. A typical combined engagement might be a website build plus the brand identity behind it plus an AI workflow that keeps the content updated. One contract, one point of contact.
How long does a project take? ▾
Web builds typically run 2–8 weeks depending on scope. AI automation and creative deliverables are usually faster — 2–7 business days. You'll get a clear timeline after I review your brief.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes. Standard packages include 2–3 revision rounds. I aim to nail the brief on the first delivery, but revisions are part of a healthy process.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
I accept payments through Fiverr, Upwork, PayPal, and direct bank transfer. Pricing is agreed upfront — no hidden fees.
Do you work with international clients? ▾
Yes, 100%. I work with clients across the US, UK, Europe, the Middle East, Canada, and Australia, fully remote and fluent in English.
What tools and tech stack do you use? ▾
React, Next.js, and Node.js for development; ChatGPT, Claude, Make.com, and n8n for automation; Midjourney, Runway, and ElevenLabs for AI content; Figma, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro for design and video.
Will I own the rights to the final work? ▾
Yes. Upon full payment, all rights to the delivered files transfer to you. I retain the right to display work in my portfolio unless you require confidentiality.
